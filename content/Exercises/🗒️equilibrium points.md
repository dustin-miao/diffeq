2. $\dot{x}=x^4-x^2$
3. $\dot{x}=\sin^2x$

> 1. The critical point occurs at $ax+3=0$, or at $x=-3/a$. If $a>0$, then the slope is negative and the point is an [[ðŸ“˜attractors|attractor]]; otherwise, it is a [[ðŸ“˜repellers|repeller]].
